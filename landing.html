Interest in IT

I can’t honestly say when my interest in IT began, because I can’t think of a time in my life where IT wasn’t intrinsic. One of my formative childhood memories involves 6-year-old me holding the Nintendo gun (NES Zapper) about one centimetre away from the convex glass TV screen, feebly trying to shoot ducks out of the pixelated sky to impress the ever-present dog (that game was truly ahead of its time). In my adult years I've been building gaming PCs by following YouTube tutorials and playing modern games with friends online facilitated by various online communication applications. Spending my entire life as a consumer and a dabbler has instilled a desire to understand it all on a deeper level (how did that NES Zapper kill those ducks? Magic?). My natural curiosity has resulted in a passion for artificial intelligence, I’m fascinated by the concept of AI and automation, and am excited by the increasingly larger role AI plays in modern society. I would love to be a player in that expanding field.

IT experience

I have no formal training in IT, but I have proven indispensable in professional environments due to my IT saturated upbringing. I was frequently leaned on as the informal “IT guy” in previous workplaces, usually to upload files to websites or troubleshoot faulty peripheral hardware, and sometimes to turn the screen on. I helped my current employer launch a successful small business which now employs me. My role in the launch was to advise the owner on which hardware to procure, to assist in the configuration of the devices and software so they were ready for use, and then finally to use the hardware and software in an ongoing daily basis in a clinical setting. That business has been successful for over a year now and I’m proud of this achievement.

Why did I choose to come to RMIT?

I chose to study at RMIT because of their impressive reputation for shaping highly employable graduates. During my studies at RMIT I expect to learn; potential career paths in the IT industry, the expectations of me as a professional in the IT industry, how to be a valuable team asset, and a variety of coding languages. After graduating, I hope to embark on a career path in the IT industry, preferably in artificial intelligence. I want to have a positive and progressive impact on society, and I believe that a solid foundation in IT is an important first step towards that goal.
